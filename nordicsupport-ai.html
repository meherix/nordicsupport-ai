How it works

Four steps, one quiet weekend.

From dropping in your manuals to having a working private assistant — most pilots are live within five working days.

01 / Ingest

Upload your knowledge

PDFs, DOCX, Markdown, Confluence, SharePoint, Notion. Files are chunked and indexed locally — never sent to a third party.

02 / Ground

Retrieve, then reason

A vector index (Qdrant or Chroma) finds the right passages. The LLM only answers from those — with citations to the source page.

03 / Act

Answer or escalate

If the answer is in your docs, employees get it instantly. If not, NordicSupport opens a structured ticket with priority, category, and a suggested fix.

04 / Learn

See your weak spots

The admin dashboard surfaces top issues, missing documents, and recurring tickets — so you can fix root causes, not just symptoms.

Frequently asked

Things teams ask before saying yes.

Where does my data actually live?

On your own server, your own VPS, or in a sovereign EU cloud you choose. NordicSupport AI does not call any external LLM API by default — the model runs locally. For air-gapped deployments, the system has zero outbound network requirements after install.

Which LLMs do you actually use?

By default, Gemma 4 (E4B for laptop-class hardware, 26B-MoE for servers) and DeepSeek V4 for higher-quality reasoning. Both are open-weight and commercially usable. We verify model licenses for your specific use before deployment, so you don't inherit any legal surprises.

Can it really speak Finnish well?

Yes — and we test it on Finnish IT vocabulary specifically (verkkokirjautuminen, etätyö, tulostinajurit, the works). For domain-heavy Finnish vocabulary, we offer optional QLoRA fine-tuning on your own corpus, kept entirely on your infrastructure.

What happens when it doesn't know the answer?

It says so — and creates a structured ticket with the user's question, suggested category, priority, and any partial answer it could find. This is by design: an honest "I don't know, here's a ticket" beats a confident hallucination every time.

How long does setup take?

A typical pilot is live in three to five working days. The longest part is usually getting your existing documents into one place — we provide tooling for SharePoint, Confluence, Google Drive, Notion, and plain folders.

Is this GDPR-compliant?

It's designed to be GDPR-aligned out of the box: data residency in the EU, right-to-erasure that actually works because data isn't copied off-site, and a Data Processing Agreement template included. Final compliance always depends on your own implementation, so we work with your DPO during onboarding.
